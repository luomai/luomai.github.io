About Me

I am an Assistant Professor in Data Centric Systems in the School of Informatics at the University of Edinburgh. Prior, I was a research associate in the Department of Computing at Imperial College London and a visiting researcher at Microsoft. My recent research focus on building large-scale and adaptive systems for diverse machine learning models and processing massive semi-structured/unstructured data in real-time. This research effort aligns with a recent big transition to the data-driven Software 2.0 era.

I am looking for PhD and MSc by Research students. If you are interested, please contact me following these hints.
