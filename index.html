About Me

I am an Assistant Professor (Lecturer) in the School of Informatics at the University of Edinburgh. I am also a visiting researcher in the Large-Scale Data & Systems Group at Imperial College London.

The goal of my research is to design future scalable and self-tuning software systems for large-scale machine learning and data processing. My current focus is building (distributed) systems that can efficiently train large vision & NLP model, graph neural networks and deep reinforcement learning models.

I have a funded PhD position and a MScR position available to start from January 2021 onwards. Candidates with strong system programming and machine learning background are encouraged to get in tonch.
Post-doc positions at Imperial College London (Topic: Machine learning systems) and University of Edinburgh (Topic: Cloud-edge AI systems).
